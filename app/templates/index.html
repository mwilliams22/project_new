Our Vision Statement.

Providing a platform for simple and convenient meal planning.

We want to facilitate meal planning for our customers and allow them to keep track of the ingredients needed for those meals

We believe that having convenient, planned out meals shouldn’t be solely available to those who readily have access to it. Our vision is to also be able to provide food to those who need it most. This is why we have decided to donate a portion of our profit to “Feed the Children”, a charity that provides meals to children around the world.
